It was 9 years ago that you took your first step into a spaceship. Now your greatest dream actually came true: exploring the universe and its creatures. On your way through the unknown, you stop at every planet you see. There! That’s a new one - this greenish shimmering globe. Shortly after landing, you meet the first inhabitant of this planet: Daxy.

He is a kind and sweet creature from the species "Daxon". They are known to be the cleanest and tidiest creatures in the universe. But Daxy is very sad. This morning, a huge storm made his food baskets spill all over the place. Now everything is unsorted and messy.
Can you help Daxy put everything in order again?


Well done! Daxy is very thankful for your help and you two decide to prepare a delicious dinner together.



Your job is to tell Daxy which of the food items are needed for the dish. One of the displayed items will have a green border around it; this is the one you need. Daxy is pretty good about understanding English, but his alien race doesn't know about locations like 'left', 'middle', or 'right', so avoid using those.
